Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This privacy policy applies to saltlife092315.com and its associated pages. It explains what happens when you visit this site. The short version: we do not collect personal information, we do not use tracking cookies, and we do not sell or share data with advertisers.

Bot verification (Cloudflare Turnstile)

Before you can view this page's content, a short automated check runs to confirm you are not a bot. This is powered by Cloudflare Turnstile. As part of this check, Cloudflare may process technical information such as your IP address and browser characteristics. This site does not receive or store that information directly. It is handled by Cloudflare under their own privacy practices. You can read Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Age confirmation

This site links to age-restricted content. You will be asked to confirm you are 18 or older before continuing. This confirmation is not stored, logged, or tied to your identity in any way. It simply unlocks the page content for that visit.

Analytics

We use GoatCounter, a privacy-focused analytics tool, to see general visit counts and which links are clicked. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not track individuals across visits, and does not collect personal data. It only records aggregate information such as approximate page views and click counts per link.

Cookies

This site does not set tracking cookies. Some cookies may be set temporarily as part of the Turnstile bot-check process; these are used only to complete that check and are not used for tracking or advertising.

Third-party links

This page contains links to third-party platforms. Once you click through, you are subject to that platform's own privacy policy and terms. This policy only covers what happens on this page itself.

Data not collected

  • Names, emails, or contact information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise location data
  • Any information you did not choose to submit
